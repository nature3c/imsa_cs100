What is the Einsenhower Matrix?
The Eisenhower Matrix is a way of time management that divides tasks into 4 categories: urgent and important, urgent but notimportant, not urgent but important, and not urgent or important
Urgent and Important
Items in this category are important and urgent such as a test or meeting that is in a week and you need to prepare
Urgent but not important
Such items in this category are things that need to get done soon but are not as important such as buying groceries
Not urgent but important
This could be something such as needing to study for a unit test that is in a month or two
Not urgent or important
Items that are not urgent or important do not need much consideration and could be activities such as watching TikTok or YouTube just for fun
