Dez Bryant

Dez Bryant is one of my biggest inspirations when it came to playing football. I remember
first watching the Dallas Cowboys with one of my cousins from Texas and seeing this receiver
catch every ball and lead the Cowboys to a playoff appearance in 2016. Desmond Bryant was
born November 4, 1988 in Galveston Island,Texas. He was a 4 star recruit in
high-school and committed to play at Oklahoma State University. After his college
football career he made the stretch to the NFL where he was drafted by the Cowboys and stayed
there for most of his career. He had over 7500 recieving yards in his 10 year career.

I remember watching Bryant's highlights and seeing him catch these impossible balls.
Watching him and Jason Witten were my biggest inspirations for getting into playing and watching
football I watched him throughout elementary and middle school but was sad when he left and was
replaced by Amari Cooper. I wanted to be like him so I started to play football more competitively.
I joined my middle school football team and eventually my high school football team. I wanted to play
like him but my lack of recieving skills led to to play linebacker and the line where I was better
suited because I could hit people hard with the strength that I had.
